Activities

Numerous pedestrian and cycle paths depart from Aste Market in the surroundings of Valli del Pasubio.

By Walking

Among the best known and most characteristic is the Via dell'Acqua ("The Water Path"). Easy path of about 10 km (round trip) to go on foot suitable for families. Along the way there are historical places such as the sawmill (which can be visited on Sunday). A picnic area allows you to spend a pleasant lunch break.



By Cycling

Within 30 km around Valli del Pasubio, many paths allow cycling activities for mountain bike lovers.

Paolo Aste knows the cycle paths of Valli del Pasubio "like his pockets". As an expert cyclist, he will be more than happy to advise you on the routes that meet your needs: from a ride with the family with children to technical circuits with demanding differences in height for the more sporty.
