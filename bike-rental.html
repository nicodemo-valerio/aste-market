Bike rental

MTB (mountain bike) bikes are available for hire for those wishing to ride one of the many cycle paths in the Valli del Pasubio area independently.

Bicycles are equipped with an electric motor that allows you to tackle climbs of any difficulty. The battery autonomy is approximately 40 km based on the type of use (Eco, Tour, EMTB, Turbo).

Small, Medium and Large sizes available.

In addition, each bicycle is supplied with a padlock and repair kit in the event of a flat tyre.

Paolo Aste knows the cycle paths of Valli del Pasubio "like his pockets". As an expert cyclist, he will be more than happy to advise you on the routes that meet your needs: from a ride with the family with children to technical circuits with demanding climbing.

Rates

  • Half day 8-13 or 13-18: 30 euro
  • Whole day 8-18: 35 euro
